## Detailed Description

GUIDify is a powerful Chrome extension designed for developers who need to generate GUIDs (Globally Unique Identifiers) quickly and efficiently.

**How to Use:**

1. Click the GUIDify icon in your toolbar
2. Enter the number of GUIDs you want (1-100,000)
3. Choose your preferred case format
4. Click "Download File" to save as .txt or "Copy to Clipboard" to copy

**Web Page Integration:**

When enabled in settings, GUIDify adds a "Load GUIDs" button next to file upload fields on any webpage. Perfect for quickly filling forms that require GUID files!

**Privacy First:**

GUIDify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All GUID generation happens locally in your browser. Your settings are stored locally using Chrome's sync storage.

**Technical Details:**

- RFC 4122 compliant UUIDs (version 4)
- Cryptographically secure random number generation
- Example format: a1b2c3d4-e5f6-4789-a012-b3c4d5e6f7a8

Perfect for developers, testers, database administrators, and anyone who needs GUIDs regularly!
